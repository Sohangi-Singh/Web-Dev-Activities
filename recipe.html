Pizza recipe

Ingridients:

  • Dough (flour, water, yeast, salt, olive oil)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (like pepperoni or veggies)

Instructions:

Start by preparing the dough using flour, water, yeast, salt, and olive oil, then let it rise until doubled in size. Roll out the dough into a circle and spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly on top.
Sprinkle generous amounts of mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings like pepperoni or veggies.
Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at around 475°F (245°C) for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Let it cool slightly, then slice and enjoy!
